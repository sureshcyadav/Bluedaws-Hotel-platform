About Us

Your Home in
Central London

Located in the heart of Paddington, Bluedaws Private Hotel offers a comfortable and convenient stay for guests looking to experience the best of the capital. Our position near Paddington Station makes arriving and getting around simple, with fast transport links to Heathrow Airport, the West End, and all of London's key districts.

Whether visiting London for business, sightseeing, shopping, or a weekend break, we combine a central location with practical comfort and great value. Our friendly team is on hand to help make your stay as easy as possible.

Getting Around

Everything Within Reach

Paddington is one of London's best-connected areas — wherever you want to go, you can get there easily.

Paddington Station

Minutes on foot — Elizabeth Line, Heathrow Express, National Rail and the Underground all from one hub.

Hyde Park & Kensington

Hyde Park, Kensington Gardens, and the museums of South Kensington are all a short walk or bus ride away.

Heathrow Airport

Direct Heathrow Express from Paddington — central London to the airport in just 15 minutes.

Oxford Street & West End

London's best shopping, theatres, restaurants, and nightlife are all easily accessible from Paddington.

Notting Hill & Portobello

The famous Portobello Road Market and Notting Hill are just a short journey from our door.

Madame Tussauds & Regent's Park

World-famous attractions including Madame Tussauds and Regent's Park are all within easy reach.
